Model Performance 

Model Performance Explained.
Precision is the ratio of true positives to the sum of true positives and false positives. 100% precision means no false positives.

Recall is the ratio of true positives to the sum of true positives and false negatives. 100% recall means no false negatives.

F1-score is the harmonic mean of precision and recall. With both precision and recall at 100%, the F1-score will also be 100%.

Accuracy is the ratio of correctly predicted instances to the total instances. With perfect precision, recall, and F1-score, accuracy will also be 100%.
