The Power BI dashboard provides a comprehensive overview of Electric Vehicle Adoption of Vehicles  manufactured between 2010 and 2024 as of the year 2024 in Washington State. Key metrics such as Total Vehicles, Average Electric Range, CAFV Eligibility, BEV Distribution, PHEV Distribution are highlighted, offering actionable insights. Visualizations include Electric vehicle type Distribution over the years, Eligibility By region and comparison of eligibility group on the basis of High, Moderate and Low.
