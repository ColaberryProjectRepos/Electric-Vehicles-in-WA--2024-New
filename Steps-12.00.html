Importing Data to PowerBI Desktop 12


ExcelWorbook


  1. Click on the Red icon Above to download Electric Vehicles Dataset
  2. Open Power BI Desktop
  3. Click on Get data ribbon.
  4.  Select Excel Workbook and then click connect
  5.  Open saved file, choose 'Electric_Vehicle_Data' file and click on Open
  6. A preview of the data will be displayed.
  7. Then click the Load Button
