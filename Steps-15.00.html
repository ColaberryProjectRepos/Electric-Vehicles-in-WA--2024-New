Creating Total Vehicles KPI(card)

  1. Go to visualization pane, click on card viz
  2. On the field values drag in Total Vehicles Measure
  3. Go to format visual tab 
  4. click on Callout Values Font style DIN  Font size  25,Category Label off
  5. Color #00000
  6. Go to General Tab adjust the measurements as follows
  7. Scroll down and on Effects turn Backgrounds off
