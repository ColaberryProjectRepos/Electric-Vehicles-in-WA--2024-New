Data Source

The data for this study was obtained from Kaggle, an official data platform, and was last updated in 2024. This dataset will be utilized to analyze insights that will inform and guide the decision-making process.

System Architecture

The project workflow involves analyzing EV adoption data to identify trends and factors, gathering and preprocessing data from data.gov, applying machine learning models to predict adoption rates, and conducting detailed analysis with DAX and Power BI. Key insights are visualized through interactive dashboards, leading to data-driven recommendations for improving adoption in low-eligibility cities.

Data Features

Key data features for analyzing EV adoption include Average Electric Range, BEV Distribution, and CAFV Eligibility indicators. Regional data such as City, State, and County help identify adoption patterns, while Make, Model, and Model Year provide insights into specific vehicle types. Total Vehicles and Eligibility Group classify vehicles based on adoption and incentive eligibility.
